Information about "Range of songs to download"

Every song on bronytunes has own number. About first 3000 songs are sorted alphabetically. Those are the oldest songs in that service. Newer songs are sortet by date added. It means that the last song is the newest.

To get actual number of the newest song go to bronytunes. Choose the newest song (top-left tile on home page) and in the adress bar you'll see something like "bronytunes.com/songs/7840" and that "7840" number is the number of the newest song.

Of course you don't have to start from first song - you can eg. download just the 100 newest songs from bronytunes.

If you want just download a few specified songs find out its numbers and go there.
